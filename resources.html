Resources!


Math & Science Resources:


Calcchat

CalcChat is a chat forum that provides interactive calculus help, calculus solutions, college algebra solutions, precalculus solutions and more. This website gives you step-by-step solutions to homework problems.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a free tutorial that teaches you lessons about a wide range of subjects.

Mathway

Mathway is a math problem solver for upper level math questions. It gives you step-by-step solutions for all your questions.

WebMath

WebMath is designed to help you solve your math problems. You fill in information and it returns and it gives you an analysis of the problem and ways to go about solving it

CoolMath

Cool Math has free online cool math lessons, cool math games and fun math activities. It has very clear math lessons




Writing Help Resources:


Grade Saver

GradeSaver will help you format your essay and paper, and also gives you tips and tricks to get a better score.

Hemingway Editor

Hemingway Editor is a website that helps you edit your papers and finds small gramatical errors. It helps your paper flow better and sound more professional.

Expresso

Expresso is a writing tool that helps you edit and improve your writing style. It teaches you to be more efficient with your writing and style



Language Resources:


Study Spanish

Study Spanish is a spanish tutoring website that provides lessons, quizzes and tests to the user. It helps with vocabulary and conjugation

Spanish Dict

Spanish Dict is a spanish dictionary. It translates phrases and paragraphs for automatic translations.

Duolingo

Duolingo is a web-based application that teaches you a variety of languages with a personalized study schedule.
