About Us


About This Website:


Studentivity was created as the final project for the Girls Who Code summer immersion program. It is designed to help students stay productive and organized. Our website is made to encourage students to work hard and stay organized with a personalized planner, email reminders, and resources to many useful homework help websites.


About The Makers:


We are Mikaelyn, Rahel, Jelani, and Selome, and we participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. For our final project, we wanted to create something that will motivate students to complete their work on time and not procrastinate. Procrastination is a problem that everyone, including us, deal with during the school year.


We believe studentivity will work to curb those procrastination tendencies.
